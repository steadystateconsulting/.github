Steady State Systems, LLC is a platform engineering, reliability engineering, and technical consulting firm focused on making critical software systems more observable, operable, scalable, and safe to change.

We work at the intersection of cloud infrastructure, software delivery, production operations, and legacy modernization — helping teams move from fragile complexity toward calm, intentional engineering.

## What We Do

Steady State Systems helps engineering teams improve the systems that carry real business weight.

Core focus areas include:

* **Platform Engineering** — internal developer platforms, CI/CD, GitOps, infrastructure automation, deployment workflows, and paved-road engineering patterns.
* **Site Reliability Engineering** — SLOs, SLIs, observability, alert hygiene, runbooks, incident response, production readiness, and operational maturity.
* **Cloud-Native Architecture** — AWS, Kubernetes, container platforms, scaling strategy, infrastructure as code, and workload reliability.
* **Legacy System Modernization** — safe migration paths, strangler patterns, operational hardening, test strategy, and incremental replacement of critical systems.
* **Technical Advisory** — architecture reviews, platform strategy, modernization planning, reliability risk assessments, and executive-facing technical translation.

## Operating Philosophy

We believe stability is not stagnation.

Good systems are designed to move, adapt, recover, and improve without losing the plot. That requires more than tools. It requires clear ownership, useful telemetry, disciplined change management, practical automation, and engineering judgment.

Our work is guided by a few simple principles:

* **Map the system before changing the system.**
* **Make failure visible before making promises.**
* **Favor boring reliability over theatrical complexity.**
* **Automate carefully, not blindly.**
* **Go slow to go fast.**

## About the Company

Steady State Systems, LLC was founded to help teams bring calm, precision, and operational confidence to systems that matter.

The firm draws from hands-on experience in platform engineering, FinTech systems, cloud infrastructure, production operations, and legacy modernization — with a strong bias toward practical solutions that can survive real operational pressure.

The brand combines three ideas:

* A **ship’s helm** — navigation, responsibility, and steady course correction.
* A **mechanical clutch or flywheel** — controlled force, smooth engagement, and engineered precision.
* A **cloud-native platform** — modern infrastructure, automation, and distributed systems reliability.

The result is the guiding idea behind the work:

**Calm. Precise. Engineered. Stable.**
